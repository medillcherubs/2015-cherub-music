Music is a large part of cherub life.

Some cherubs are also talented musicians. But even the tone-deaf among them bonded over shared musical tastes. Whether they were lounging in the dorms at 1 a.m., working on the trend story in Fisk or relaxing on the beach on a lazy Saturday afternoon, cherubs could be found dancing with the music blasting.

“The second floor has been watching High School Musical a lot together. We all sing along to the soundtrack. We belt it at the top of our lungs. I don't think Mariah has anything on us.” – Jennifer Kusch

“From listening to it practically on loop during one of the broadcast labs to learning about and watching the musical "The Million Dollar Quartet," I had rock-and-roll on my mind a lot more than usual these five weeks.” - Alex Schwartz

“I was playing it while getting ready in the morning, and my roommate said he liked it. We proceeded to have a conversation about music. To put this moment in context, I can count the number of conversations we had on one hand.” – Liam Kirchner

“This Korean song helped Ashley and me bond. We both like this song very much. Whenever we listen to this song, we turn the volume up very loudly and dance crazily to the rhythm.” – Winny Sun

“The song talks about Chicago and one of the lyrics is "fireworks on Lake Michigan." Someone played it during the 4th of July at the lakefill.” – Maggie Harden
